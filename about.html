About Us

Our Founder: Precious Radebe

Precious Radebe is a passionate advocate for social empowerment and community development. With a vision to uplift and support those in need, Precious founded Empowering the Nation in 2018.

Our Mission

Empowering the Nation was established with a singular purpose: to provide domestic workers and gardeners in South Africa with the skills and knowledge they need to improve their lives and the lives of their families.

In a country where many individuals in these roles face significant challenges and lack opportunities for professional development, Precious saw a need for a program that could offer practical training and support. By focusing on skill-building and education, our organization aims to create pathways to better job opportunities, financial stability, and personal growth.

Why We Were Created

The idea for Empowering the Nation was born out of Precious's deep understanding of the struggles faced by domestic workers and gardeners. Many of these individuals work tirelessly but still find themselves in precarious financial situations due to a lack of formal skills and training. Precious believed that by offering targeted upskilling programs, we could make a tangible difference in their lives.

Our programs are designed to:

  • Enhance Skills: Provide training that can lead to better job performance and increased earning potential.
  • Foster Personal Development: Support personal growth and confidence-building through various workshops and resources.
  • Create Opportunities: Open doors to new career paths and professional advancement.

Through Empowering the Nation, Precious Radebe and our dedicated team are committed to making a positive impact, one individual at a time. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential, and we are here to make that happen.

Thank you for visiting our page and learning more about our mission and values.
